Motivations, Abilities, and Personality

Motivations

You are motivated to study difficult puzzles, solve big problems, and conquer tough challenges. Exciting missions at work will involve discovering unique answers, making critical decisions, and starting new ventures. You might be conflicted at times between simultaneously wanting to spend the time necessary to analyze a problem, be in on all phases of a project, and lead a new project. Keeping motivated will involve finding resolution to these tensions so your energy is positively directed toward activities that matter to you. One key for you might be to think like a strategic deal-maker. This will have you balance the need to understand the details with the pressures of closing the deal.

Abilities

Your primary competencies are detailed analysis, strategic thinking, and creative thinking. As such, you will be drawn to teams in your organization that concentrate on Intelligence, Strategy, and Development. You will likely pride yourself on your ability to solve complex problems, be a smart planner, and create new products and services.

Personality

Your dominant traits will cause you to be a person who is thorough, demanding and adventurous. This will play well in environments that are all-encompassing and difficult like high-level engineering, the military, and intelligence work. You will constantly have high expectations of yourself and the people around you as you work diligently to meet challenges and obstacles head-on. However, you will likely be quite intense. People around you can find you over-powering and tough. You can be combative and insensitive at times and you'll always stand ready with a detailed explanation for any problems that occur. Being defensive, however, can get you into trouble. Learn to develop productive outlets for your tensions so that you aren't perceived as reactive. Also, be careful to not be thought of as impetuous or else superiors will make you "wait your turn" by playing control games.

Key Strengths

Very Inquisitive

You are very curious and like to solve complex and intricate questions. Always wanting to know why something happens will have you explore facts and question reasons for things.

Accomplishment-Oriented

You are a practical-minded person who wants to see tangible accomplishment from your efforts. Hands-on and no-nonsense in your approach will have you be responsible and effective.

Highly Motivated

You are driven to succeed. You will take on challenge and overcome obstacles in order to achieve your goals and gain the rewards that come from winning.

Caution Areas

Might Over-analyze Everything

You want all the details before coming to conclusions. This may cause you to have difficulty with stopping your research and coming to a decision.

Likely Frustrated When Not Seeing Results

You work diligently to get things done on your own, but could be frustrated when working with others if things take too long. Be careful of showing your frustrations inappropriately.

Might Tend to Compete with People

Your drive and courage may have you compete to win, and you may fail to see when this approach is inappropriate to a given situation.
